Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I get a Google AI Studio API key?

Visit Google AI Studio, sign in with your Google account, and click "Create API Key". Copy the key and paste it in the Settings panel of this app.

Q: Is this free to use?

The app itself is free. However, you need a Google AI Studio API key, which has a free tier with rate limits. For heavy usage, you may need to pay for API calls. Check Google AI Pricing for current rates.

Q: What types of PDFs work best?

PDFs with selectable text work best. This includes most digital documents, research papers, and ebooks. Scanned PDFs (images of text) won't work as they require OCR, which this tool doesn't currently support.

Q: How long does processing take?

Processing time depends on document length, batch size, and API rate limits. A typical 50-page document might take 5-15 minutes. Enable Turbo Mode in Settings to process multiple batches in parallel and speed things up.

Q: What happens if processing fails?

The app automatically retries failed batches and can failover to backup API keys and alternative models. You can also download partial results at any time, or retry failed batches manually after adjusting settings.

Q: Can I customize the transformation?

Yes! Go to Settings â†’ Prompts to customize how the AI transforms your text. You can adjust the system prompt, transformation instructions, and specific prompts for tables, code, math, and figures.

Q: Is my data secure?

Yes. All processing happens in your browser. Your API key is stored only in LocalStorage, and files are stored only in IndexedDB (both local to your browser). The only external communication is with Google AI Studio to process text.

Q: Can I use this offline?

No. The app requires an internet connection to communicate with Google AI Studio's API. However, once a document is processed, you can access the stored results offline.

Q: What's the difference between models?

Different Gemini models have different capabilities and costs:

  • Flash models: Faster and cheaper, good for most documents
  • Pro models: More capable, better for complex technical content
  • Experimental models: Latest features, may be unstable
The app tries models in priority order and automatically fails over if one doesn't work.

Q: I'm getting rate limit errors. What should I do?

Try these solutions:

  • Increase "Rate Limit Delay" in Settings
  • Reduce "Parallel Chunks" or disable Turbo Mode
  • Add a backup API key for automatic failover
  • Wait a few minutes and retry
  • Consider upgrading your Google AI Studio plan

Q: Can I process multiple files at once?

Currently, the app processes one file at a time. However, you can queue multiple files by processing them sequentially. Batch processing multiple files simultaneously may be added in a future update.

Q: How do I report bugs or request features?

Visit the Contact page or open an issue on our GitHub repository. We welcome feedback and contributions!

Q: Can I export to formats other than PDF?

Yes! After processing, you can download the transformed text as a plain text file. EPUB and Markdown export options are planned for future releases.
